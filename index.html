Hi, I’m Allie. I was born and raised in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia, and now I live in Chicago.

I started doing hand-lettering projects in an effort to preserve and sustain the art of hand writing and letter writing after spending two years working for Smithsonian’s National Postal Museum in Washington DC, where I spent entire days photographing thousands of incredibly beautiful, rare, and historic specimens of hand-lettering and epistolary design. I feel strongly that the written word carries with it an immensely communicative and distinct experience that should not be lost, and I'd love to work with you on a custom project.
